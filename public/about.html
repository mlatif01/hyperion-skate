    About

    HYPERION was a Titan god, who was a warrior that conspired with Kronos but was later defeated by Zeus and cast into Tartarus. Hyperion swore to have his vengeance on the god's and has been fighting ever since…

    Hyperion represents the warriors code. Honour, integrity, strength, discipline, confidence and bravery.

    These are attributes that only a few hold, to really enjoy the fruits of your labour one must understand what it's like to hit rock bottom but have the discipline, confidence and bravery to get back up and keep on fighting.

    With this mind set you can achieve your goals and reach above your dreams, The name Hyperion literally translates into "he who goes above".

    It's a mind set.
